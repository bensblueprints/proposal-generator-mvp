# Product Hunt launch — Pitchcraft

**Name:** Pitchcraft

**Tagline (60 chars):** Proposal software you buy once — no PandaDoc subscription

**Description (260 chars):**
Build proposals from blocks, add a pricing table with client-toggleable add-ons, send a trackable link, and collect a click-to-sign acceptance. Self-hosted (Docker) or desktop app. Your data, your server, $29 once — instead of $19+/user/month forever.

**Full description:**
Pitchcraft is a self-hosted proposal and quote builder for freelancers, agencies, and small studios.

- Block-based editor: cover, text, pricing, terms, testimonials, images — drag to reorder
- 6 starter templates so your first proposal takes minutes, not an evening
- Pricing tables where optional add-ons are client-side toggles — the client upsells themselves and the total updates live before they sign
- Trackable share links: opens, view count, total reading time
- Click-to-sign acceptance with a stored record: signer name, timestamp, IP, and the exact total including chosen add-ons
- Request-changes comments straight from the client page
- Optional email notifications through your own SMTP
- Runs as a Windows desktop app or on any $5 VPS with Docker; data is one SQLite file

No per-seat pricing, no monthly fee, no vendor holding your sales pipeline hostage. MIT-licensed source on GitHub; $29 gets you the packaged 1-click installer.
